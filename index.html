S3: Pushing the boundaries of
human knowledge in the field of ML

We are a group of individuals passionate about answering some of the most intriguing questions in the field of Machine Learning. Founded by Dr Gagan Raj Gupta, S3(Sigma-Cubed) is the primary research group of IIT Bhilai that focuses on research in various domains of Machine Learning and Data Science.

Smart Solutions for the Society

We want to develop smart solutions to address problems in healthcare, agriculture and other areas such as preventing road accidents, improving sanitation and quality education for all.

Simple Solutions by Students

In this theme, we want to encourage our student community to develop empathy for others and experience the joy of selfless service. We can participate in developing simple, frugal yet effective solutions to problems around us. These may or may not involve AI or computers. We will teach courses, run a reading group, invite guest speakers, hold tutorials and practice sessions to help each other learn

Scalable Smart Systems

In this theme, we want to help build solutions that scale to process large amounts of data. Applications can be in industry, research, social use etc.
