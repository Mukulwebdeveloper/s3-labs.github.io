Do ML models preserve privacy?

Differential Privacy

ML has provided a lot of consumer applications in the past decade. But all that data being fed into ML models needn't necessarily maintain the privacy of the contributor. Differential Privacy focuses on how datasets can be described without giving away user info to the model.

Do we really need a 1000 dimensions?

Dimensionality Reduction

The monster called ML needs to be fed data. Datasets can have as many as a million features. But are all of those important? Dimensionality Reduction aims to reduce the dimensions of a dataset while still preserving the important information.
